INGREDIENTS:

  • 1/2 of a ripe Avocado
  • 1 tablespoon of rolled oats(Oatmeal smoothes the skin)
  • 1 tsp of raw honey(Properties in honey will unclog pores, moisturizing properties will sooth the skin and give it a glow)
  • 1 tsp of jojoba oil(Great for sensitive skin, balances oil production on skin)
  • 1 tablespoon of coconut oil(Cleanses skin while moisturizing)

HOW TO:

    1. Combine all ingredients in your blender and blend until smooth.

    2. Wash face with warm water. Lay a warm wet washcloth on your face for 2 minutes to open pores.

    3. Apply mask to face. Leave on for 20 minutes then wash away with warm water.

    4. Once all of the mask is removed from your skin, place a cool wet washcloth on your face for 2 minutes to close the pores.

BENEFITS:

    Oatmeal can soak up the excess oil on your skin and help treat acne. Its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory

    properties help to treat dry skin and remove dead skin cells. Oats also contain compounds called saponins,

    which are natural cleansers. They remove the dirt and oil that clog the pores and exfoliate the skin.
